How to play 👀 SNEAK PEEK GAME 👀?

Both players close their eyes at the start of the game.
The goal is to catch the other player sneak peeking! which means,
➊ You open your eyes to find the computer’s eyes closed --> game continues
➋ You open your eyes to find the computer’s sneak peeking --> you win!
➌ The computer opens its eyes to find that you’re sneak peeking --> you lose :(
➍ Remember to keep your eyes closed as much as possible. Give you opponents less chance to win!

Close and open your actual eyes to control the eyes in the game. Hope your eyes get a little break from the screen while playing this game. Happy gaming!
